✨Achievements of Sabrina Carpenter✨

Sabrina Carpenter has released several successful albums and hit songs like “Nonsense” and “Feather.” She has performed on big stages around the world and opened for Taylor Swift on the Eras Tour. Her music has reached millions of listeners, and she’s known for her strong vocals, creative lyrics, and stylish performances. Sabrina has also been featured in major magazines and continues to grow as one of today’s top pop artists.

Sabrina has won:

  • Record of the Year: For "Espresso"
  • Album of the Year: For "Short n’ Sweet"
  • Song of the Year: For "Please Please Please"
  • Best New Artist
  • 2025 Winner Grammy: Best Pop Solo Performance
